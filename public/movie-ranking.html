The Best Movies According to Marius

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spiderman 3

From all the orignal Spiderman movies, one through to three, this one was my favourite.

Iron Man

Iron Man, although being an old movie, was peek entertainment in my childhood. I probably watched this movie 100 times.

Extraction

A much more recent movie, but amazing all the same. I rate both number 1 and 2 as a must watch!

Honorable mentions.

The Equalizer

An amazing action movie set, The Equalizer 1 and 2 are heartwarming action movies about a retired CIA agent, anonomously helping regular people against evil gangs and corrupted officials.

Django

Man... I don't know what to say. This movie brought tears to my eyes. This movie reminds us about the brutal nature of slavery and how the character Django get's a taste of freedom. All the freedom he needed in order to fight for said freedom and rescue his wife broomhilda from a brutal slaver.
